About Internship Fair

Internship Fair is an initiative by the Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) , TIET which as the name suggests, seeks to provide young minds an ability to grow working alongside with their academics with a proper certification in hand. The internship fair entertains all students from the institute with a certain skillsets and lead them in a proper professional direction providing guidance.

Companies from all backgrounds, core and non-core take part to make this fair a success. This includes manufacturing, hands on experience, telecommunication, real estate, branding, marketing, media and every other field in which a head start may play an influential role. The application process for every company is individually decided through the representatives of the company.

CV is a major deciding factor since screening through a short interview and an attractive portfolio is what usually makes a difference. Over 250 internships were offered in 2019 through the fair and over 400 applications were processed.
